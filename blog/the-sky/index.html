The Sky and Everything Therein

August 15, 2023

We aren't really strangers, are we? Far from it.

And yet—in this last embrace, I question whether it was ever a possibility. After all, this universe and all matter within has collapsed to the state of our current reality. Inherently, the life that we now live holds no more weight than the neighboring reality. The crossing of our paths in the way that we know it was only one of an infinite possibilities: all identically probabilistic in nature.

But I question what it all means.

I'll always look back on this time with a heartwarming nostalgia; my eyes opened. For being the best friends one could hope to have, I owe you no less than the sky and everything therein. Because—you have gifted me the ability to truly see it.
