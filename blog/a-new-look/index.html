A New Look

August 13, 2023

"There is grandeur in this view of life, with its several powers, having been originally breathed into a few forms or into one; and that, whilst this planet has gone cycling on according to the fixed law of gravity, from so simple a beginning endless forms most beautiful and most wonderful have been, and are being, evolved."

- Charles Darwin, On the Origin of Species

Over the past few days, I've overhauling this website. If you were a regular here before, then I'm sure you'll notice quite the difference. I quite like it. Change is good: how else can you grow?

As always, this website was built completely by me with plain 'ol HTML and CSS. At this point, I haven't really attempted to integrate anything else (although some scripts not be out of the question in the future). It may not be the fanciest thing ever, but at least it's authentic. I didn't just follow some step-by-step YouTube tutorial to build this.

Even if this was a complete overhaul, it took less time to complete than when I developed it the first time ~5 months ago. Which—I mean—is to be expected, but is also a sign of growth.

It's not purely a visual change, though, this update also serves to add mobile fucntionality to my home on the web. Good news—this means you can read my blogposts from anywhere! Even your Nest Hub (whatever that is)!

Also, I do think that this rendition is organized more cleanly as well (on account of me not being a complete noob), so expansion should be relatively painless.

Anyway, that's all for now. I move into my dorm in Austin on the 16th, so expect a new post around then. In case you're especially interested, I've hidden a clue concerning its topic somewhere in this page.
